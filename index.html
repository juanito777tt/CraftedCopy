Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

Monthly retainer with custom scope, or a fixed project to build the system. We’ll align on cadence during the call.

What’s included?

System install (ICP, sheets, messaging) + a prospecting playbook. Optionally, we operate the pipeline for you. We use your Apollo/Sales Nav/Clearbit seats, or include a pass-through data budget if you prefer us to host.

Who is this for?

Funded startups (Seed–Series B), agencies (marketing/creative/B2B) scaling past 6–7 figures, and expert operators/consultants with high-ticket offers.

What’s the outreach method?

High-ticket = low volume, high relevance. Cold email + LinkedIn with 4 consultative touches: (1) insight + audit invite, (2) case study/framework, (3) “noticed X — considered Y?” pattern-interrupt, (4) final nudge (limited capacity). Each touch references live triggers (funding/press/hiring).

What’s the commitment?

Minimum two months to build, implement, and calibrate your system. We limit intake to 3 new clients per quarter.

Any requirements?

We don’t work with businesses under $15k monthly revenue or without an active sales process/team.

Data & tools — do you include subscriptions?

We install the machine on your stack. You provide access to Apollo/Sales Nav/Clearbit (preferred), or we add a pass-through data budget if you want us to host the seats. No reselling of data.

Exclusivity & intake

Operator-led studio, no juniors. We cap at 3 new clients per quarter to protect quality and speed.
