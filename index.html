Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

7-Day Sprint: Campaigns $4,000 or Flows $4,500 (flat, scope-locked). Retainers: Lite $2,500/mo; Plus $3,500–$4,500/mo.

What’s included?

Recurring (Lite): 2 campaigns/week (strategy, copy, design, ESP build, QA), 1 focused A/B test/month (rotating), bi-weekly deliverability checks, and monthly KPI reporting. Plus: everything in Lite + 1 heavier flow iteration/month (new branch/offer) and segmentation work.

Who is this for?

Ecom brands between $50k–$500k/mo with lists of 10k+, using Klaviyo/ConvertKit/Mailchimp, and able to start within 4 weeks. Below that, we’ll recommend a Starter Sprint or a partner.

What’s the cadence & SLAs?

Monthly calendar approved in Week 0. Assets locked 48h before send. Up to 2 revisions per campaign. Reporting monthly (CTR, CVR, revenue/email, deliverability).

What’s out of scope?

More than 2 campaigns/week, more than 1 test/month, full new flows (use the Sprint), ESP migrations/builds at scale, and daily/weekly deliverability tinkering (we do bi-weekly).

How do you handle tests?

One focused A/B per month on retainer (rotate: welcome subject/preheader, popup creative/offer, abandon-cart timing/cadence). In sprints: one subject/CTA test (campaigns) or two flow split tests.

Commitment & capacity

Sprint is 7 calendar days. Retainers are month-to-month after the first month. We limit intake to protect quality and inbox placement.

Do you refer out if we’re not a fit?

Yes. If revenue <$50k/mo or needs are outside email (paid ads, CRO, SMS-only, large-scale ESP migration), we’ll make warm intros to vetted partners within 24h.
